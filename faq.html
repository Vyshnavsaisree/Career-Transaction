FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about the job portal and application process.

How to prepare resume?
Keep it concise, highlight relevant skills and achievements, use clear formatting, and tailor it to the job description. Proofread for errors.
How to say self introduction?
Briefly mention your name, background, key skills, and what you are looking for. Keep it professional and relevant to the job.
How to shift domain?
Identify transferable skills, take relevant courses or certifications, update your resume, and network with professionals in the target domain.
How to negotiate notice period?
Communicate your reasons clearly, check company policy, offer solutions (like early handover), and be polite and professional during discussions.
